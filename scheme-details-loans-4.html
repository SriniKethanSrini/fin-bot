National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC) Scheme

Description:

The National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC) offers various loan schemes to support Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs). These schemes include the Raw Material Assistance Scheme, Single Point Registration Scheme, and Credit Support Scheme, providing financial assistance, raw material procurement support, and credit facilitation to small businesses.

How to Apply for the Scheme:

Visit the official website of the National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC) or contact your nearest NSIC office for information on loan schemes and application procedures. You may need to submit an application along with required documents to avail of the benefits under NSIC schemes.
