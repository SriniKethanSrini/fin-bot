Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme (CLCSS)

Description:

The Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme (CLCSS) is launched by the Ministry of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) to provide financial assistance to small-scale industries by facilitating technology upgradation with the introduction of improved machinery.

How to Apply for the Scheme:

Visit your nearest bank branch participating in the CLCSS scheme or contact the designated nodal agency for the scheme. You will need to submit a project proposal along with relevant documents to avail of the benefits under CLCSS.
