Central Government Employees Pension Scheme (CGEPHIS)

Description:

The Central Government Employees Pension Scheme (CGEPHIS) is a contributory pension scheme for central government employees, including those working in autonomous bodies and Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs). It provides a regular pension to employees after retirement.

How to Apply for the Scheme:

Central government employees are automatically enrolled in the CGEPHIS scheme upon joining service. They contribute a portion of their salary towards the pension scheme during their employment. Pension benefits are disbursed through the central government's pension disbursement system upon retirement.
