Central Government Health Schemes

Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana (RSBY)

Description:

RSBY provides health insurance coverage to below-poverty-line (BPL) families for hospitalization expenses.

How to Apply for the Scheme:

Visit your nearest government health center or contact the RSBY helpline for enrollment and registrationlink for apply. You will need to provide proof of identity and BPL status to enroll in the scheme.
