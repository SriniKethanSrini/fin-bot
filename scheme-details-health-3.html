Central Government Health Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY)

Description:

PMSSY focuses on upgrading and establishing tertiary healthcare facilities across India.

How to Apply for the Scheme:

PMSSY projects are implemented by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare. Visit the official PMSSY website for project details and application procedureslink for apply. You may need to submit project proposals or applications for funding under PMSSY.
