Central Government Health Schemes

National Rural Health Mission (NRHM)

Description:

NRHM aims to improve healthcare delivery in rural areas, providing accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare to pregnant women, newborns, and children.

How to Apply for the Scheme:

Contact your nearest Anganwadi Center or Primary Health Center (PHC) for information on available healthcare services and programs under NRHM. You may need to visit the nearest health center for medical check-ups and registrations under NRHM.link for apply online .
