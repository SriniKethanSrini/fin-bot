Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Housing for All (Urban)

Description:

The Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Housing for All (Urban) scheme aims to provide affordable housing to urban poor by offering interest subsidy on home loans. Beneficiaries under the scheme can avail of subsidized home loans from banks and financial institutions for constructing or purchasing a residential property.

How to Apply for the Scheme:

Visit your nearest bank branch participating in the PMAY scheme or contact the official PMAY website for more information on loan application procedures and eligibility criteria. You may need to provide necessary documents such as income proof, identity proof, and address proof during the application process.
