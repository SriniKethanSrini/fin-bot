Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Scheme Details

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) is a Government of India initiative focused on addressing the declining Child Sex Ratio (CSR) and empowering the girl child. It emphasizes raising awareness about the value of girls in society, promoting their education, and ensuring their protection. Through various programs and initiatives, BBBP aims to tackle gender inequality and discrimination against girls, ultimately fostering a more equitable and inclusive society. While individuals typically do not apply for this scheme directly, they can contribute to its objectives by participating in awareness programs, volunteering, supporting girl child education, and advocating for gender equality.

How to Apply

To apply for the Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) scheme, individuals typically do not apply directly as beneficiaries. Instead, the scheme is implemented by the government through various programs and initiatives aimed at improving the welfare of the girl child and promoting gender equality.

Various Programs
