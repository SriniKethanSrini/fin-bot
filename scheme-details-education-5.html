Balika Samriddhi Yojana Details

Description

This is a scheme aimed at promoting the education and welfare of the girl child in India. It includes various incentives and support systems to encourage families to educate and nurture their daughters.

How to apply

As the "Balika Samriddhi Yojana" is no longer an active scheme and has been subsumed into other programs like the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS), you can't apply for it specifically. However, if you're looking for schemes or initiatives aimed at the welfare and development of the girl child in India, you may consider exploring other ongoing government programs and schemes.

Check Eligibility Criteria: Once you identify relevant schemes, carefully review the eligibility criteria to determine if you qualify for participation. Eligibility criteria may vary depending on the specific scheme and its objectives.

Government officials at these offices can provide you with information and support regarding available schemes and the application process.
