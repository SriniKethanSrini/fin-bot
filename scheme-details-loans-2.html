Stand-Up India Scheme

Description:

The Stand-Up India Scheme aims to promote entrepreneurship among women and Scheduled Castes (SC) and Scheduled Tribes (ST) by providing loans for setting up greenfield enterprises in manufacturing, services, or trading sectors.

How to Apply for the Scheme:

Contact your nearest bank branch participating in the Stand-Up India Scheme or visit the official Stand-Up India website for more information on loan application procedures and eligibility criteria.
