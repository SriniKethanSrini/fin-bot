National Pension System (NPS)

Description:

The National Pension System (NPS) is a voluntary, defined contribution retirement savings scheme designed to enable systematic savings during the subscriber's working life. It offers market-linked returns and flexibility in choosing investment options and pension fund managers.

How to Apply for the Scheme:

To enroll in the National Pension System (NPS), individuals can visit authorized Points of Presence (POPs) such as banks, pension funds, and other financial institutions. They need to submit the required documents along with the filled application form and make the initial contribution to open an NPS account.
