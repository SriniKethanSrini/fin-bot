Employees' Provident Fund (EPF)

Description:

The Employees' Provident Fund (EPF) is a mandatory savings scheme for employees in the organized sector. Both the employer and employee contribute a percentage of the employee's salary to the EPF account. The accumulated amount provides financial security post-retirement.

How to Apply for the Scheme:

Employees automatically become members of the EPF scheme upon joining a company covered under the EPF Act. The employer deducts the employee's contribution and contributes it to the EPF account along with the employer's share. Employees can check their EPF balance and manage their account through the EPFO portal or mobile app.
